# Alpha Star Aviation Services – Comprehensive Data Collection

## Company Overview

### Formation and Base
Alpha Star Aviation Services is a private charter airline founded in **2010** in **Riyadh, Saudi Arabia**.  
The company operates from **King Khalid International Airport** and provides **domestic and international charter services**.  
**Source:** alphastarav.com (Home page – English)

### Mission
Alpha Star’s mission is to provide **best-in-class flight services**, maintain **high safety standards**, and establish **long-term partnerships** with clients, staff, and business partners.  
The company also aims to deliver **fast, safe, and specialized medical evacuation services** with the highest standards of care.  
**Source:** “Mission” section – About Us page

### Vision
Alpha Star’s vision is to be a **benchmark in aircraft management and the private aviation business**.  
**Source:** “Vision – Being a Benchmark”

### Values
